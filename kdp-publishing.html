Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about publishing with us.

What is KDP publishing?
Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing lets you self-publish eBooks and paperbacks for free and sell them worldwide.
Do I keep all my rights?
Yes, you keep full copyright and control of pricing and distribution at all times.
How long does publishing take?
Once approved, your book usually appears on Amazon within 24–72 hours.
What formats are supported?
KDP accepts eBook and paperback files in formats like PDF and EPUB for quick upload.
Is ISBN required?
For paperbacks, Amazon provides a free ISBN or you can use your own; eBooks don’t require one.
