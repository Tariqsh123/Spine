Professional KDP Book Publishing Services

At Author Publishing Experts, we help authors bring their stories to life on Amazon KDP. From formatting to publishing, our team ensures your book meets Amazon’s standards while maximizing visibility. We’ve supported countless authors with affordable KDP publishing services, helping them successfully launch their books to a worldwide audience.

KDP eBook Publishing

We handle the entire process of publishing your eBook on Amazon KDP — from manuscript formatting to metadata setup — ensuring your book is ready for millions of readers.

Paperback Publishing

Our experts prepare your paperback files according to Amazon’s print requirements, including layout, trim size, and ISBN setup, making sure your book is print-ready.

Book Formatting & Layout

We create clean, professional interior layouts for both eBooks and paperbacks, ensuring your content is easy to read and meets Amazon’s publishing guidelines.

Amazon SEO & Metadata Setup

We optimize your book’s title, keywords, and description to improve visibility on Amazon, helping you attract more readers and boost your sales potential.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced KDP Specialists

Our team has years of experience publishing books on Amazon KDP. We guide you through every step — from formatting to publishing — ensuring your book meets industry standards and stands out on Amazon.

End-to-End Publishing Support

Whether it’s eBook or paperback, we handle the technical details like formatting, cover setup, ISBN, and metadata, so you can focus on your writing while we make your book publishing-ready.

Global Reach & Visibility

We optimize your book for Amazon search, helping you reach readers worldwide. From keyword selection to category placement, we maximize your chances of getting discovered and increasing sales.

Affordable & Author-Friendly

We offer cost-effective KDP publishing services tailored to independent authors. Our goal is to help you publish with confidence, retain your royalties, and build a long-term author brand.
