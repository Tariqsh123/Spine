Publishers Based in Britain Who Understand Authors First

We understand how overwhelming the process of self-publishing books can feel. From confusing requirements to endless delays, authors often struggle to navigate the system.

That’s why our team at Self Publish Books UK focuses on removing the stress! Our team of professionals offers expert guidance, practical support, and a clear path, ensuring your book reaches readers smoothly and confidently.

Book Publishing Solutions That Put You in Control

Most book publishing companies make aspiring authors feel powerless, with slow timelines, hidden costs, and limited control being the norm. Many writers end up frustrated, waiting years for their work to see the light of day.

As trusted England book publishers, we change that narrative!

Our model ensures that authors retain 100% royalties, while our streamlined approach delivers results 3x faster, ensuring your book can be published in just 8–12 weeks.

On top of that, you hold 100% creative control, from the cover design to the final edits. We’re not here to dictate your vision but to bring it to life with professional support, industry expertise, and a commitment to making publishing fair and empowering for every author.

Simplifying Online Book Publication Across Britain

Our professional self publishing help is rooted in a deep understanding of the UK publishing industry. With team members across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland—from London and Manchester to Edinburgh and Birmingham—we provide localised support.

Self Publish Books UK team ensures your book is perfectly positioned for UK readers, with native British English editing, seamless UK ISBN registration, and insights into regional reading preferences that generic international services simply cannot match.

Your trusted partner in publishing. Helping authors succeed with expert book services and global reach.
