How quickly can I publish my book with you?
Our process is designed for speed. Unlike traditional methods, we complete publishing in 8–12 weeks. This means authors can publish in Britain market faster, without waiting years for approval.
Will I keep the rights to my book?
Yes, you always keep full rights. Unlike many traditional book publishing solutions, we ensure authors retain control, ownership, and royalties while still benefiting from professional services that meet publishing standards in the UK.
What editing support do you provide?
Our team offers developmental editing, copy-editing, and proofreading. This ensures every manuscript meets the highest professional standards. These services set us apart from other UK self book publishing companieswho often skip detailed editorial support.
Do you help with distribution across the UK?
Yes, we arrange nationwide distribution. With our services, your book reaches bookshops, online retailers, and libraries across the country, giving authors a strong presence in theUK market.

Traditional publishers often take 18–24 months to release books, keep most royalties, and control decisions. Our approach gives authors faster timelines, higher royalties, and independence, while still delivering professional UK publishing industry standards.

Absolutely. We support writers across the UK, including Manchester, Birmingham, Edinburgh, and Cardiff. Our nationwide presence helps authors anywhere enter the established UK publishers market with expert guidance and professional backing.
