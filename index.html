Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Can I try Spines for free?
Yes! Anyone can sign up for a free account by clicking the “Get Started” button at the top of the page. Try out our platform tools and see how easy it is to proofread, design your cover, and publish your book with Spines.
How long will it take to publish my book with Spines?
With Spines, the entire publishing process typically takes around 30 days, from the moment you choose your plan until your book appears on Amazon.

Keep in mind, the timeline can vary depending on your needs. If you choose to work with our professional team for additional services—like translation, line editing, illustration, ghostwriting, and more—your publishing journey may take a bit longer to ensure top-quality results.
How much does publishing with Spines cost?
Spines offers three standard publishing plans, as well as two specialized plans for authors focused on children’s literature. Our plans are designed to fit different publishing goals, budgets, and book formats.

The cost varies depending on several factors, such as the length of your manuscript, the formats you choose to publish (eBook, audiobook, softcover, or hardcover), how much personal support you’d like along the way, and any additional services you may require.

Some authors want a simple, streamlined path to get their book live on Amazon. Others want a full-service experience, including translation, multi-format publishing, and hands-on guidance from our team. Wherever you fall on that spectrum, there’s a plan built to meet you right where you are.

To find the best fit for your book and your vision, explore our pricing page or connect with one of our publishing experts for personalized guidance.
What payment methods can I use?
We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment options may vary depending on your location.
What if I change my mind?
You can request a cancellation or plan change at any time. Refund eligibility depends on the stage of your publishing process and the services already provided.
Does Spines support any languages other than English?
Yes! We offer publishing in multiple languages and can connect you with professional translators for both fiction and non-fiction works.
How do royalties work?
Authors receive a percentage of each book sale, depending on the publishing plan and sales channel. You’ll have full transparency through your Spines dashboard to track your earnings in real-time.
Before uploading my manuscript to Spines' platform, what format should it be in?
We recommend submitting your manuscript in Microsoft Word (.docx) or PDF format. This ensures the smoothest processing and editing experience.
What other services do you offer?
In addition to publishing, we offer translation, editing, illustration, cover design, audiobook production, marketing campaigns, and more.
